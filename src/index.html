10 Major Changes in Philadelphia in 10 Years

Data Visualization February 12, 2019
Read time: 5 min
Philadelphia, like many U.S. cities, has a unique story to tell. Over the past ten years, the city has undergone profound change: population has been growing every year, becoming younger, more diverse, and more dynamic; employment, once a big concern for local officials and residents, continues to increase; and construction cranes and new high-rise buildings dot the city skyline. But these changes bring new issues, and the perennial challenges of poverty, crime, and high taxes remain persistent. In honor of The State of the City’s 10th year, we’d like to paint the portrait of Philadelphia through 10 compelling data points—pulled from each chapter theme in the report—that spotlight the transformative trends in the city over the past 10 years. Some topics are ripe for a deeper exploration to better understand the data and how Philadelphians are impacted.
